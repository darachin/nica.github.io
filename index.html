Statement from His Excellency Secretary of State of State Secretariat of Civil Aviation

State Secretariat of Civil Aviation (SSCA) is a government body responsible for all civil aviation activities in the Kingdom of - Cambodia especially security, safety in order to support the growth of air transportation. National Institute of Civil Aviation(NICA), under the management of SSCA is facilitating for training to all SSCA officials and civil aviation private sector per- sonnel.

We are fully aware of the importance of the civil aviation sector and its role as a component of the Kingdom of Cambodia economy providing possibilities to boost the economic growth through creating employment opportunities, facilitating world trade, stimulating vital economic sectors such as tourism, trade, industry...etc. For such purposes, we are keen to commit to work within the framework of the royal government strategy and its short and long term plans to develop human resource, in line with Civil Aviation Training Center vision. Such commitment is through implementing training programs and workshops to ensure the achievement of human, economic, social, and envi- ronmental sustainable development, keeping up with the country trends and strategic projects to upgrade the civil aviation sector in terms of developing facilities, service quality to meet the international standards.

On the behalf of the Royal Government of Cambodia, May I express my appreciation to Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA), especially the government and the people of the Republic of Korea for the continuous supports to Cambodia in many fields such as hospitals, education centers and infra- structure. In specific I would like to show my gratitude to Korean Ambassador and KOICA manager who have been working hard to achieve this 10,1 million dollars project happened and continue to provide technical support with experienced experts from Korea Airports Corporation (KAC).

Since the establishment, many objectives have been achieved according to plan and reached its dateline throughout the process. As the Cambodian Royal Government strategy focuses on the development of human resources, Civil Aviation Training Center will be playing an important role to train SSCA officials and civil aviation personnel to meet the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) standards and to meet the need of growth of air transportation in the region especially an open sky policy as part of ASEAN Civil Aviation sector.

We will continuously devote by supporting from KOICA, NICA will reach our expectation as setting in our master plan. So we are looking forward to cooperate in developing Cambodia as well as strengthening our partnership. tnership. NICA

Thank you!!

H.E Dr. Mao Havannall

MINISTER
STATE SECRETARIAT OF CIVIL AVIATION





Message from President

National Institute of Civil Aviation(NICA) of Secretariat of Civil Aviation (SSCA) in Cambodia is a specialized training center for civil aviation training which is established from Grand Aids Funding by the Republic of Korea, through Korean International Cooperation Agency (KOICA) in October 2013 striving to enhance professional skills for Cambodian civil aviation and establishing an efficient man- agement system for human resources.

Since the establishment of this training center in October 2016, many courses have been added and many skills 1 have been attained during the process. Therefore, in the up-coming year ahead we hope to achieve our strategy plan.

It is an honor to obtain a new and renovated institution, which is equipped with the latest equipment and tools. CATC of Cambodia aims to accomplish the objectives and reached the demand from the State Secretariat of Civil Aviation (SSCA) and the services provider such as Cambodia Air Traffic Services (CATS), Cambodia Airport Management Services (CAMS) and Airline Companies with all aspects.

We as a team will continuously devoted to develop NICA and expand its institution professionally and well-known not only in the nation but also globally prominent

Thank you!!

H.E Lim Kao

PRESIDENT
NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF CIVIL AVIATION
